What are the main skills and competencies that a Project Manager should have?

A Facilitator
is a person or thing that makes an action or process easy or easier.
For example: Would you help an old lady or an old man to carry the groceries home?

A Multitasking Talent
is someone or something that performs multiple tasks at the same time.
For example: Are you able to stay concentrated on doing your homeworks while listening to a podcast or to music?

A Public Speaker
is a person who gives speeches, lectures or presentation to a usually large group of people.
For example: Do you find it easy to perform a presentation in front of the class?

A Team Player
is a person who plays or works well as a member of a team.
For example: Do you prefer to work in a group or alone to deliver a work result?

A Decision Maker
is a person who makes important decisions.
For example: Do you find it easy to make decisions that also have an impact on others?

A Communicator
is a person who is able to convey or exchange information, news, or ideas
For example: Do you find it easy to summarize content and highlight the essentials?
